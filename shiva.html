Title : 7 Secrets of Shiva

Author: Devdutt Patanaik

"7 Secrets of Shiva" by Devdutt Pattanaik is an enlightening exploration of Hindu mythology and philosophy through the lens of Lord Shiva. Pattanaik unveils the symbolic layers of Shiva's narratives, decoding the seven secrets that define the deity's essence. With a blend of storytelling and scholarly insights, the book delves into Shiva's roles as the destroyer and creator, the ascetic and householder, unraveling profound wisdom and universal truths. Pattanaik seamlessly intertwines mythology with contemporary relevance, offering readers a deeper understanding of Shiva's significance in Hinduism and beyond. This insightful guide provides a captivating journey into the timeless wisdom encapsulated in the myths surrounding Lord Shiva.
