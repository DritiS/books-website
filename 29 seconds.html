Title : 29 Seconds

Author: T M Logan

"29 Seconds" by T. M. Logan is a gripping psychological thriller that explores the dark side of power and moral dilemmas. Sarah, a university professor, is offered a chance to erase a life-altering harassment situation in exchange for a favor. As she grapples with the ethical implications, the novel delves into the complexities of morality, justice, and the consequences of compromising one's principles. Logan masterfully weaves suspense and tension, keeping readers on the edge of their seats as they navigate the blurred lines between right and wrong. With well-drawn characters and a thought-provoking narrative, "29 Seconds" is a compelling exploration of human nature and the choices we make under pressure.
