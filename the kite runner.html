Title : The Kite Runner

Author: Khaled Hosseini

"The Kite Runner" by Khaled Hosseini is a poignant exploration of friendship, redemption, and the impact of personal choices. Set against the backdrop of Afghanistan's turbulent history, the novel follows Amir, a privileged boy, and Hassan, his loyal friend from a different background. Their lives take divergent paths after a fateful event, leading Amir on a guilt-ridden journey seeking redemption. Hosseini weaves a compelling narrative, delving into themes of loyalty, betrayal, and the haunting legacy of the past. Through intricate storytelling, the novel captures the complexities of human relationships, providing a powerful and emotional glimpse into the Afghan culture and the universal quest for forgiveness.
