Title : The Mysterious Affairs at Styles

Author: Agatha Christie

Agatha Christie's "The Mysterious Affair at Styles" introduces readers to the iconic Hercule Poirot in his debut case. Set in the English countryside during World War I, the novel follows Captain Hastings as he assists Poirot in solving a murder at the Styles estate. The intricate plot unfolds with clues, red herrings, and Poirot's brilliant deductive skills. Christie's mastery of the whodunit genre shines through as she crafts a suspenseful narrative with a diverse cast of characters. This classic mystery, marked by meticulous plotting and clever twists, establishes the foundation for Christie's illustrious career as the Queen of Crime.
