Title : Harry Potter

Author: J K Rowling

J.K. Rowling's "Harry Potter" series follows the extraordinary journey of a young wizard, Harry, as he navigates the magical world of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. From his discovery of being a wizard to the epic battle against the dark wizard Voldemort, the series spans seven books, each unveiling layers of mystery, friendship, and bravery. Rowling's rich storytelling introduces readers to a captivating realm filled with spells, creatures, and moral complexities. "Harry Potter" transcends its fantasy genre, addressing universal themes of love, courage, and the battle between good and evil, captivating readers of all ages with its enchanting narrative.
