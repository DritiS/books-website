Title : Sherlock Holmes

Author: Sir Arthur Conan Doyle

Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's "Sherlock Holmes" series follows the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes and his loyal companion Dr. John Watson as they solve intricate mysteries in Victorian London. Holmes, known for his deductive reasoning and keen observation, tackles perplexing cases, often involving crime, intrigue, and enigmatic characters. Doyle's masterful storytelling and Holmes' iconic methods make the series a classic in detective fiction. From the famous "Hound of the Baskervilles" to "The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes," the novels and short stories showcase Holmes' unmatched intellect and enduring appeal, creating a literary legacy that has captivated readers for over a century.
